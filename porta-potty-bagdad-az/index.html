How much does a standard porta potty rental cost?

Average porta potty rental prices range from $85–$130/week. Monthly rates for long-term standard portable toilet rental start around $275–$350. Final cost depends on location and service frequency, especially in high-demand states like Texas or Florida.

Do you offer same-day delivery?

Yes. Same-day porta potty rental is available in most metro areas in California, Florida, Texas, Georgia, and Arizona, subject to availability.

How many units do I need for a 150-person wedding?

General rule: 1 unit per 50 guests for a 4–6 hour event. For 150 people, 3–4 standard units or 1 deluxe trailer is recommended nationwide.

Are your ADA units available for residential use?

Yes. ADA compliant portable toilets are available for home events, medical needs, or accessibility requirements in all 50 states.

What’s the difference between deluxe and trailer rentals?

Deluxe portable toilet rental is a single upgraded stall with better interior finishes. Porta potty trailer rental is a multi-stall, climate-controlled unit with real plumbing,deal for 100+ guests.

Bagdad, AZ Insights: Population,
Zip Codes, Influence, and Service Areas

Bagdad is a copper mining community and census-designated place (CDP) in Yavapai County, Arizona, United States, in the western part of the state. It is one of only two remaining company towns in Arizona. The population was 1,876 at the 2010 census, up from 1,578 in 2000.
